Italian Mechada Sandwich

The Italian Mechada sandwich features tender rib meat braised in a flavorful mix of white wine and a rich beef base. After cooking to perfection in a pressure cooker, the meat is shredded and served on toasted bread with creamy avocado, fresh tomato slices, and a touch of mayonnaise.

This hearty sandwich offers a perfect balance of savory shredded beef and fresh toppings, making it a satisfying and classic Chilean dish.

Ingredients

  • 45 gr of vegetable oil
  • 1 kg of rib meat
  • 1 Tablespoon of Gourmet ground beef base
  • 375 ml cold water
  • 125 ml white wine

Steps

For the base for carne mechada

  1. Add 3 tablespoons (45g) of vegetable oil to a pot over high heat. Once the oil is hot, add 1kg of rib eye meat, brown until sealed on all sides.
  2. Dissolve the sachet (45g) in 375ml of cold water (approx. 1 ½ cup) and 125ml of white wine (approx. 1/2 cup). Pour the prepared Gourmet Shredded Beef Base into the pot with the meat and mix well.
  3. Cook over low heat for 1 ½ hours in a pressure cooker or 2 ½ hours in a conventional pot.
  4. Once the meat is tender, cut into slices or shred. Once ready, serve immediately.

For the sandwich

  1. Cut the bread and brown it with butter in a frying pan.
  2. Then serve the shredded meat with avocado, tomato, and mayonnaise.
